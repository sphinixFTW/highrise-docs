<Tab value="Asset Store">
Unity's Asset Store is full of pre-made assets waiting to be used. To find them:

1. Navigate to Unity's Asset Store by clicking on `Window > Asset Store`.
2. Search for the desired asset online.
3. Select the asset you want.
4. Import the chosen asset directly into your project.
5. Enjoy the convenience of seamless integration!

<Tab value="Manual Import">
To import locally stored files:

1. Right-click within the Project panel.
2. Select `Import New Asset`.
3. Browse to the location of your desired file.
4. Click to import the selected file.
5. Enjoy easy importation of locally stored assets!

<Tab value="Drag & Drop">
An effortless way is to directly drag and drop files from your Explorer/Finder into Unity's Project panel.

1. Open your Explorer (Windows) or Finder (Mac).
2. Locate the files you want to import into Unity.
3. Click and hold on the desired files.
4. Drag the files over to Unity's Project panel.
5. Release the mouse button to drop the files into Unity.
6. Watch as Unity automatically imports the dragged files into your project.
7. Enjoy the simplicity of drag-and-drop asset importing!
